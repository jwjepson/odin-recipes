Cinnamon Rolls

Recipe courtsey of Ambitious Kitchen

Description

This cinnamon roll recipe is absolutely delicious. The rolls come out very soft and fluffy, filled with brown sugar filling and glazed with a vanilla cream cheese glaze. These cinnamon rolls make a perfect dessert for the entire family, and trust me, they'll be asking for more!

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons quick rise or active yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk, at room temperature
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 3 cups bread flower, plus more for dusting
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened

For the cream cheese frosting:

  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 3 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Warm milk to around 110 degrees F. Transfer warm milk to the bowl of an electric mixer and sprinkle yeast on top. Add in sugar, egg, egg yolk and melted butter. Mix until well combined. Next stir in flour and salt with a wooden spoon until a dough begins to form.
  2. Place dough hook on stand mixer and knead on medium speed for 8 minutes, until it forms into a ball, but it still slightly sticky.
  3. Transfer dough to oiled bowl, and cover with plastic and a warm towel for 1 hour to 1 1/2 hours until the dough has doubled in size.
  4. Transfer dough to floured surface and roll into a 14x9" rectangle. Spread softened butter over the dough, leaving a 1/4 inch margin.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar and cinnamon. sprinkle the mixture over the buttered dough and rub the brown sugar into the butter with your hands.
  6. Roll up the dough tightly starting from the 9 inch side, and make sure to cut off an inch of dough from both ends, after rolled up.
  7. Slice the rolled up dough into 1 inch sections. This should give you about 9 rolls
  8. Place the cinnamon rolls in a greased 9x9 baking pan, lined with parchment paper. Cover with plastic wrap and warm towel and let the rolls rise for another 30-45 minutes.
  9. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Remove plastic wrap and towel and bake for 20-25 minutes, until slightly golden brown.
  10. For the frosting: In an electric mixer (if you have one), combine the cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract. Beat with a paddle attachment until smooth and fluffy. Spread over the cinnamon rolls and serve right away!
