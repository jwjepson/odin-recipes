Pizza Dough

Recipe courtsey of Sally's Baking Addiction

Description

This delicious pizza dough is incredibly soft and fluffy, with a delicious taste. This recipe is extremely versatile, in that it can be made thin and crispy, or thick and fluffy. Better yet, it doesn't take many ingredients to make, and is so simple that anyone can make it!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups (320ml) warm water
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (7g) active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon (13g) granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons (30ml) olive oil
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 1/2 cups (450g) all purpose flour
  • Sprinkle of cornmeal for dusting the pan

Steps

  1. Whisk warm water, yeast, and granulated sugar in stand mixer with dough hook attachment. Cover with a towel and allow to rest for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the olive oil, salt, and flour to the bowl. Beat on low speed for 2 minutes. Take out the dough and put onto floured surface and knead for 3-4 minutes, until dough is soft. Poke it with your finger, if it bounces back, it's ready! Otherwise, keep kneading.
  3. Lightly grease a large bowl and place the dough inside. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and a warm towel, and allow dough to rise for 60-90 minutes.
  4. Preheat oven to 475 degrees F, allowing the oven to heat for 15-20 minutes. Lightly grease a baking sheet and sprinkle with cornmeal. This step is optional, but recommended!
  5. After the dough has risen, punch it down to release air, and divide the dough in half. One a lightly floured surface gently flatten the dough into a disc shape. Place the disc onto the baking sheet and with floured hands, gently stretch into a 12 inch circle. Pinch or fold over the edges to create a thick crust.
  6. Brush the top of the dough lightly with olive oil, and using your fingers put dents into the surface. Top with your favorite sauce and toppings and bake for 12-15 minutes.
  7. Serve immediately. Enjoy!
